Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about planning with us.

How early should I book an event planner?
For weddings, we recommend booking 6-12 months in advance. For corporate events, 3-6 months is ideal. However, we do handle short-notice events based on availability.
Do you handle events outside of your base city?
Yes, we are destination event specialists and handle projects across India and international locations.
What is your payment structure?
We typically require a booking deposit, followed by milestone payments as we finalize vendors and designs.
